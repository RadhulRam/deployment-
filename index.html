Our Schemes

Fixed Deposit

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a financial instrument where an investor gives a certain sum of money to a bank or a financial institution (company) and the entity pays interest for the duration of the deposit.

Monthly Income

A monthly income scheme is a financial instrument where an investor gives a certain sum of money to a bank or a financial institution (company) and the entity pays interest for the duration of the deposit.

Reccuring Deposit

The Recurring Deposit (RD) scheme in the Nidhi where an investor deposits a fixed amount with monthly intervals for a fixed tenure (mostly ranging from one year to five years).

Daily Deposit

Daily Deposit scheme is a financial instrument where an investor gives a certain sum of money to a bank or a financial institution (company) and the entity pays interest for the duration of the deposit.

Savings Account
